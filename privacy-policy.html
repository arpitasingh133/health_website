Sharing of Information

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical information to third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing with healthcare providers or labs

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authorities
  • Medical Emergencies: In life-threatening situations where immediate medical attention is required

Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform:

Authentication Cookies: Maintain your login session and ensure secure access
Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform to improve it

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active and you continue to use our services
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be retained for up to 3 years after account inactivity, unless deletion is requested
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with medical record keeping requirements or legal obligations
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for research and improvement purposes
