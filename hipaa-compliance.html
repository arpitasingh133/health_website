HIPAA Compliance

How VDocs complies with HIPAA to protect your health information.

1. Security Measures

We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards including encryption, access controls, employee training, and regular audits to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your Protected Health Information (PHI).

2. Business Associate Agreements

We require all third-party vendors and partners who handle PHI on our behalf to sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs), ensuring they follow HIPAA regulations.

3. Patient Rights

You have the right to access your health information, request amendments, and receive a record of disclosures. We honor these rights in line with HIPAA mandates.

4. Breach Notification

In the event of a breach of unsecured PHI, we will promptly notify affected individuals, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), and other required parties as outlined by HIPAA’s Breach Notification Rule.
