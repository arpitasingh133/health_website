Refund Policy

Last updated:

General Policy

All purchases and subscriptions made through VDocs are subject to our refund policy. We aim to provide clarity and fairness when it comes to refunds.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds may be requested within 7 days of purchase for annual subscriptions only.
  • Refunds are not applicable for services that have already been used or consumed.
  • All refund requests must be accompanied by a valid reason.

Processing Time

Once approved, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days. The refunded amount will be returned to the original payment method.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to request a refund, please contact our support team at support@vdocs.in.
