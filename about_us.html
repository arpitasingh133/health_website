Empowering Your Health Journey

Why We Exist

Fragmented care: Many patients face difficulties managing health documents across clinics, labs, and hospitals.

Lack of continuity: Important test histories or prescriptions are often unavailable at the point of care.

Data vulnerabilities: Paper records and unprotected systems expose sensitive medical data to loss or misuse.

Our Approach

AI-assisted management: Intelligent tools to help users organize and retrieve records (available in phases).

Encryption-first: End-to-end encryption and user-controlled sharing for enhanced privacy and confidentiality.

ABDM-ready architecture: Built with support for integration with India's Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.

OUR STORY

From personal challenge to healthcare solution

VDocs was inspired by a real-life experience of navigating India's healthcare system without easy access to past medical records. What began as a personal mission has now grown into a collective effort by engineers, doctors, and data privacy experts to reimagine health record accessibility.
